User Research

A reflection on the value gained by gathering user feedback in playtests. As the game designers and developers, we were too close to the game and ended up having a few blind-spots to its shortcomings. Thanks to our playtests and user research, we were able to identify those issues and address them before the final deliverable.

Dog Movement

Our dog movement became smooth and natural looking because our users pointed out the choppy movement it had.

Player Sprite Hitbox

The player's hitbox became clear because users pointed out that they were colliding with walls too early.

Directions

The map became easier to navigate because users mentioned getting lost during the game-play and struggling to find the spaceship before their oxygen ran out.

Door Labels

Users said they did not know how to unlock the doors, how close they were to unlocking a door, or the order in which doors would unlock, so we created labels for each door.
